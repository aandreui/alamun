Security Council

The Security Council has primary responsibility for the maintenance of international peace and security. To achieve this, it takes the lead in determining the existence of a threat to the peace or act of aggression. It calls upon the parties to a dispute to settle it by peaceful means and recommends methods of adjustment or terms of settlement.
