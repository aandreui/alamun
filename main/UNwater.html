UN Water

In 2003, UN-Water was established as an interagency mechanism to coordinate the efforts of UN entities and international organizations to sustainably manage water and sanitation all around the globe. They accomplish this in three ways: “Inform Policies”, “Monitor & Report” and “Inspire Action”.
