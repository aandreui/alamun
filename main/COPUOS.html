COPUOS

The Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space (COPUOS), was established in 1958 as an ad-hoc committee of the United Nations in order to discuss the arising situations of space exploration and its use, approved formally as a stable body in 1959. Since then it has been closely related to the United Nations Office of Outer Space Affairs (UNOOSA) which provides the secretariat services to this committee.
